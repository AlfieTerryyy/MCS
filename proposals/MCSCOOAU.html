The MCS Codex of Order and Unity

ITEM 1: Emergency Powers Act
Justification: For the safety and stability of the server during emergencies.
Allows the server leader to take temporary absolute control in case of a crisis, such as a "server invasion," "resource depletion," or "economic instability."
ITEM 2: Efficiency Governance Act
Justification: To streamline decision-making and reduce legislative gridlock.
Permits a smaller "Executive Committee" to make critical decisions without requiring a full parliamentary vote.
ITEM 3: National Resource Oversight Law
Justification: To ensure fair use of server resources.
Creates a centralized authority to oversee all resources, with the government as the "fair and impartial managers." All players must report their resource earnings and get permission to build large projects.
ITEM 4: Security Protocol Mandate
Justification: To protect the server from internal and external threats.
Requires all server members to register their weapons, potions, and TNT usage with a "security council" Non-compliance leads to confiscation.
ITEM 5: National Infrastructure Act
Justification: To improve server infrastructure for everyone's benefit.
Places all major roads, portals, and community farms under government direct management, granting government control over server movement and resources.
ITEM 6: Taxation and Resource Redistribution Act
Justification: For equality and fairness.
Implements a tax on high-value resources (diamonds, netherite) and redistributes them as "rewards" for loyalty or good behavior, managed by the council.
ITEM 7: The Anti-Corruption Initiative
Justification: To ensure transparency in governance.
Allows the "anti-corruption task force" to investigate anyone suspected of corruption. This can be used to discredit political rivals.
ITEM 8: Historical Preservation Decree
Justification: To honor the server's history.
Grants government control over areas deemed "historically significant," blocking others from altering key server zones.
ITEM 9: Censorship Protection Law
Justification: To maintain a positive environment.
government authority to remove "toxic" chat messages or dissenting opinions under the guise of server harmony.
ITEM 10: Server Election Integrity Act
Justification: To ensure fair representation.
Introduces complex voting procedures controlled by government, such as requiring "eligibility tokens" that you distribute to ensure only "committed" players can vote.
ITEM 11: Knowledge Centralization Act
Justification: To create a server library for everyone's benefit.
Requires all enchanted books, maps, and written books to be stored in a "central library" managed by the government. Players can only borrow them temporarily with explicit permission.
ITEM 12: Defense Contribution Act
Justification: To prepare the server against external threats (e.g., raids, PvP events).
Mandates regular contributions of weapons, armor, and rare resources to a "defense fund," controlled by the government. as community effort to ensure safety.
ITEM 13: Environmental Preservation Act
Justification: To protect the natural beauty of the server.
Establishes protected areas where mining, building, and deforestation are prohibited to maintain the server's ecological balance.
ITEM 14: Trade Regulation Act
Justification: To ensure fair and consistent trade practices.
Requires all trades above a certain value to be documented and reported to the "Trade Council" for approval.
ITEM 15: Education and Training Initiative
Justification: To improve the skills and knowledge of server members.
Introduces workshops and training sessions for tasks like redstone, building, and PvP techniques.
ITEM 16: Server Unity Celebration Act
Justification: To promote community bonding and morale.
Establishes a monthly event where players gather for games, challenges, and server-wide builds.
ITEM 17: Server Defense Coalition
Justification: To organize collective defense efforts.
Forms a coalition of trusted players to strategize and respond to external threats like raids or griefers.
ITEM 18: Creative Innovation Act
Justification: To encourage unique and innovative builds.
Introduces a reward system for players who create outstanding builds or contribute innovative ideas to the server.
ITEM 19: Economic Stability Initiative
Justification: To stabilize the server's economy.
Implements a standardized currency system and regulates the prices of rare resources.
ITEM 20: Freedom of Creativity Act
Justification: To ensure players can express themselves freely.
Protects players' rights to build and create freely, as long as their projects do not conflict with established laws or harm the server's harmony.
